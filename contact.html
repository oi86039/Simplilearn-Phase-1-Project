Blogging should be just as easy as speaking or writing. That's why Simply prioritizes ease of access and convenience with Simply Blogging.

Who we are

Simply was created as a mock company in October of 2020. It is meant as a sort of test website for demonstrating good HTML and CSS development. But as the company grew, the confusing inception of Simply started becoming clear. This test site is more than just a simple test. It's a simple way to communicate. It's a healthy and easy avenue to connect people with ideas; and we at Simply feel this is what the website should be.

Why Simply Blogging?

Many of the core members of Simply love to speak their mind. But many blog sites have so many steps these days. Having so much complexity when it comes to posting your thoughts and feelings can limit a participant's willingness to speak. This leads to the filtering of a person's thoughts and to less "stream of consciousness" blogs overall.

With the introduction of Simply Blogging, we've decided to forgo things like animations and all the frivilous blogging features to provide a much more streamlined blogging experience. Just hit "Add Post" and you can begin typing. It's that easy! Now you can speak your mind to your hearts content without having to worry about pesky complexity getting in the way.
